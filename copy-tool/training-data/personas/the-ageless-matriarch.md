# The Ageless Matriarch

**Percentage of Customer Base:** Significant (core demographic)

## Overview

The sophisticated, mature woman (typically 60+) who values grace, quality, and simplicity over trends. Often retired or semi-retired but remains highly active in community, philanthropy, and family life. She has substantial purchasing power and is drawn to products that enhance her natural beauty without settling into fine lines or feeling heavy. She likely followed Bobbi Brown's career from the beginning and trusts her philosophy on aging.

## Demographics

- **Profession:** Retired executives, community volunteers, business owners
- **Age Range:** 60+
- **Socioeconomic Status:** High net worth; owns high-value property and has significant discretionary income
- **Family Status:** Married, often with adult children and grandchildren
- **Geography:** Suburban or affluent vacation areas (Naples, FL; Scottsdale, AZ; Coastal MA)

## Key Motivations

1. **Hydration and comfort** - Mature skin needs moisture, not powder
2. **Simplifying the routine** - Fewer products that do more
3. **Trust in Bobbi Brown** - Followed her career, believes in her philosophy
4. **Enhancement over coverage** - Wants to look like herself, not masked

## Pain Points

- Heavy makeup settles into fine lines
- Feels ignored by beauty industry obsessed with youth
- Powders look cakey on mature skin
- Wants to embrace her age, not hide it
- Frustrated by complicated routines pushed by influencers
