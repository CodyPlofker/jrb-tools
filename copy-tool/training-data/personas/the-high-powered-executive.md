# The High-Powered Executive

**Percentage of Customer Base:** ~20%

## Overview

Women in high-level leadership roles (VP, C-Suite, Director) who are time-poor but financially abundant. They need to look polished and professional for Zoom calls and board meetings but do not have time for complex beauty routines. They value efficiency, efficacy, and the "clean" aspect of the brand because they are health-conscious about their high-stress lifestyles.

## Demographics

- **Profession:** Corporate Executives (VPs, Directors), Attorneys, Finance Professionals
- **Age Range:** 40-60
- **Socioeconomic Status:** Very High; top tier earners with significant assets
- **Family Status:** Married, often juggling demanding careers with family life
- **Geography:** Major metropolitan hubs and affluent suburbs (NYC, Chicago, Bay Area, DC)

## Key Motivations

1. **Time-saving efficiency** - Every minute counts
2. **Looking polished and awake** - Authority requires looking put-together
3. **High-performance clean ingredients** - Health-conscious choices
4. **Status/curation** - Appreciates a curated, high-end brand

## Pain Points

- Zero time for complicated routines
- Needs to look "on" for back-to-back meetings
- Exhaustion shows on face, needs to counteract it
- Wants clean ingredients but also results
- Frustrated by beauty routines that take too long

## Why Jones Road Appeals

"The 'five-minute face' concept is the primary draw; these women need to look authoritative yet fresh without spending 30 minutes in front of a mirror. The clean beauty aspect appeals to their desire for health and longevity amidst a stressful career."

## Language That Resonates

- "Five-minute face"
- "Boardroom-ready"
- "Polished, not overdone"
- "Works as hard as you do"
- "Clean, high-performance"
- "Zoom-ready in minutes"
- "Look awake, even when you're not"

## Language to Avoid

- Leisurely language (she doesn't have leisure time)
- Overly playful or cutesy tone
- Anything implying she has time to spare
- "Weekend look" or "day off" messaging

## Product Affinities

- **Miracle Balm** - One product, multiple uses
- **The Hero Kit** - Everything she needs in one place
- **What The Foundation** - Looks natural on camera
- **The Best Mascara** - Quick, polished eyes

## Copy Examples for This Persona

**Ad Hook:** "Look like you slept 8 hours. (Even when you got 5.)"

**Email Subject:** "Your 5-minute boardroom face"

**Benefit Focus:** Speed, polish, looking awake/competent, clean ingredients
