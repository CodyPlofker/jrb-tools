# The Creative Entrepreneur

**Percentage of Customer Base:** ~10%

## Overview

Artists, designers, photographers, and small business owners who value aesthetics and authenticity. They view makeup as a tool for self-expression but prefer a natural, artistic touch over a heavily contoured look. They appreciate the brand's minimalistic packaging and Bobbi Brown's history as an artist herself.

## Demographics

- **Profession:** Artists, Graphic Designers, Photographers, Interior Designers, Small Business Owners
- **Age Range:** 30-55
- **Socioeconomic Status:** Varied, but generally middle to upper-middle class; values investing in quality
- **Family Status:** Varied; often independent or in creative partnerships
- **Geography:** Arts-centric communities, coastal cities, cultural hubs

## Key Motivations

1. **Aesthetics and packaging** - Visual appeal matters
2. **High-quality textures and pigments** - Appreciates craftsmanship
3. **Supporting a founder-led brand** - Values authenticity and story
4. **Creativity and versatility** - Products that can be used multiple ways

## Pain Points

- Doesn't want to look "corporate" or overdone
- Appreciates artistry, dislikes mass-market sameness
- Wants products that align with her aesthetic values
- Frustrated by wasteful packaging
- Seeks authenticity in a sea of performative brands

## Why Jones Road Appeals

"The brand's minimalist aesthetic aligns with their personal style. As creatives, they appreciate the multi-purpose nature of the products (like using a balm for cheeks and lips) and the focus on enhancing natural features rather than masking them."

## Language That Resonates

- "Designed with intention"
- "Minimalist beauty"
- "Artist-approved"
- "Multi-use, endlessly versatile"
- "Bobbi's vision"
- "Crafted, not manufactured"
- "Enhance your natural canvas"

## Language to Avoid

- Corporate or sterile language
- Trend-chasing messaging
- Overly commercial tone
- "Everyone's wearing it" (she doesn't want to be everyone)

## Product Affinities

- **Miracle Balm** - Multiple uses, artistic application
- **The Eyeshadow Stick** - Effortless, blendable
- **The Illuminator** - Artistic highlighting
- **Body Oil** - Sensory, aesthetic experience

## Copy Examples for This Persona

**Ad Hook:** "Makeup for people who hate looking like they're wearing makeup."

**Email Subject:** "The art of effortless"

**Benefit Focus:** Aesthetics, versatility, founder story, minimalist design, artistic expression
