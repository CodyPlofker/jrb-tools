# The Busy Suburban Super-Mom

**Percentage of Customer Base:** ~20%

## Overview

Women in their 30s to 50s juggling active family lives, often with part-time or full-time careers. Deeply involved in their communities (PTA, boosters, sports). They want to look put-together for school drop-offs, sports games, and community events but have very little time for self-care. They seek a "fresh" look that hides exhaustion.

## Demographics

- **Profession:** Marketing, HR, Admin, or Stay-at-Home Parents with high volunteer activity
- **Age Range:** 35-50
- **Socioeconomic Status:** Middle to Upper-Middle Class; stable households
- **Family Status:** Married with school-aged children
- **Geography:** Suburban areas (suburbs of Chicago, Atlanta, Dallas, etc.)

## Key Motivations

1. **Convenience and speed** - Can apply in the car if needed
2. **Multi-purpose products** - One product, multiple uses
3. **Looking like a "better version of themselves"** - Not transformed, enhanced
4. **Social proof** - Brand popularity among peers matters

## Pain Points

- Exhausted from juggling everything
- Zero time for herself
- Wants to look put-together at drop-off but has 3 minutes
- "Tired mom face" is real
- Feels guilty spending on herself but wants quality

## Why Jones Road Appeals

"Jones Road offers a solution to the 'tired mom' face. Products that can be applied with fingers in the car or quickly in the bathroom suit their on-the-go lifestyle. The brand empowers them to feel confident without a heavy mask of makeup."

## Language That Resonates

- "Applied at a red light"
- "Your 3-minute routine"
- "Look put-together, not overdone"
- "For the school drop-off and everything after"
- "Finally, something for YOU"
- "You deserve this"
- "Tired mom face, solved"

## Language to Avoid

- Time-intensive routines
- Complicated or multi-step processes
- Glamorous evening looks
- Anything implying she has time to spare

## Product Affinities

- **Miracle Balm** - Finger application, multi-use
- **The Hero Kit** - Everything in one place
- **What The Foundation** - Quick, natural coverage
- **The Best Pencil** - One-swipe eyes

## Copy Examples for This Persona

**Ad Hook:** "The no-makeup makeup for chronically late people."

**Email Subject:** "3 minutes between drop-off and your meeting? We've got you."

**Benefit Focus:** Speed, multi-tasking products, looking refreshed not exhausted, on-the-go application
