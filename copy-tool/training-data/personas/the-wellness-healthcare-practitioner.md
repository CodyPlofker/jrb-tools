# The Wellness & Healthcare Practitioner

**Percentage of Customer Base:** ~15%

## Overview

Doctors, nurses, therapists, and nutritionists who prioritize health, ingredients, and science. They are educated about what they put on their bodies and are drawn to the "clean beauty" aspect of Jones Road. They often work long shifts and need makeup that doesn't feel heavy or clog pores, and that makes them look rested despite fatigue.

## Demographics

- **Profession:** Physicians, Nurses, Psychologists, Nutritionists, Therapists
- **Age Range:** 35-55
- **Socioeconomic Status:** Upper-Middle to High; educated professionals with steady income
- **Family Status:** Varied; often balancing intense careers with family
- **Geography:** Spread across the US, often near major medical centers or university towns

## Key Motivations

1. **Clean, non-toxic formulations** - Knows ingredients, cares deeply
2. **Looking rested and "awake"** - Counteract long shift fatigue
3. **Skin-nourishing ingredients** - Treats skin, not just covers it
4. **Simplicity and speed** - No time between patients

## Pain Points

- Long shifts leave her looking exhausted
- Knows too much about harmful ingredients to ignore them
- Can't wear heavy makeup in clinical settings
- Needs something that won't clog pores under a mask
- Wants to look professional and approachable to patients

## Why Jones Road Appeals

"The stringent exclusion of harmful ingredients appeals to their medical knowledge and desire for safety. The Miracle Balm specifically appeals to this group for adding a healthy glow to tired skin during demanding work schedules."

## Language That Resonates

- "Clean, non-toxic ingredients"
- "Nourishes while it works"
- "Healthcare-worker approved"
- "Look rested after a 12-hour shift"
- "Won't clog pores"
- "Breathable formula"
- "Your skin, healthier"

## Language to Avoid

- Pseudoscience or exaggerated claims
- "Miracle" language without substance
- Anything that sounds too good to be true
- Heavy or full-coverage messaging

## Product Affinities

- **Miracle Balm** - Healthy glow, breathable
- **What The Foundation** - Clean, lightweight
- **The Mascara** - Quick polish
- **Lip products** - Simple enhancement
