# JRB Launch Tiering System

## Tier Definitions by Business Category

| Tier | Revenue Target | Launch Scale | Frequency | Launch Types |
|------|---------------|--------------|-----------|--------------|
| **Tier 1** | $5MM+ | Hero | Max 4/year | Product Launch Campaign, Integrated Brand Campaign |
| **Tier 2** | $2.5MM - $5MM | Major | ~6/year | Product Launch Campaign, Possible Integrated Brand Campaign |
| **Tier 3** | $500K - $2.5MM | Standard | ~6/year | Product Launch Campaign, Shade Extension, GWP |
| **Tier 4** | Under $500K | Minor | No set frequency | Product Launch, GWP, Shade Extension |

### Shoot Requirements

#### Tier 1 - Large, Multi-Shade Complexion Campaign
- Professional Model Shoot (3-4 Days)
- Professional Product Shoot (1-2 Days)
- In-House Product Photo/Video Shoot with Props (~2 Weeks)
- Production Video Shoot (1 Day)

#### Tier 1 - Holiday
- Professional Model Shoot (2 days)
- Professional Product Shoot (1-2 Day)
- In-House Product Photo/Video Shoot with Props (~3 Weeks)
- Production Video Shoot (1 Day)

#### Tier 2 - Multi-Shade Launch (>6 Shades)
- Ben Ritter Shoot (2 Days)
- Professional Product Shoot (1 Day)
- In-House Product Photo/Video Shoot (~2 Weeks)

#### Tier 2 - Multi-Shade Launch (<5 Shades)
- Ben Ritter Shoot (1 Day)
- Professional Product Shoot (1 Day)
- In-House Product Photo/Video Shoot (~1 Week)

#### Tier 2 - Exclusive Kits/Limited Edition Products
- Ben Ritter Shoot (<1 Day)
- In-House Product Photo/Video Shoot with Props (1-2 Weeks)
- *No Professional Product Shoot*

#### Tier 3 - Multi-Shade Launch (>6 Shades)
- Ben Ritter Shoot (2 Days)
- Professional Product Shoot (1 Day)
- In-House Product Photo/Video Shoot (~2 Weeks)

#### Tier 3 - Multi-Shade Launch (<5 Shades)
- Ben Ritter Shoot (1 Day)
- Professional Product Shoot (1 Day)
- In-House Product Photo/Video Shoot (~1 Week)

#### Tier 3 - Kit Relaunch/GWP
- In-House Product Photo/Video Shoot (1 day)
- *No Professional Shoots*

#### Tier 4 - Shade Extension
- Professional Product Shoot if we need to reshoot the family with new shades (.5 day)

#### Tier 4 - Super Small Launch (e.g., Powder Puff)
- In-House Product Photo/Video Shoot (1 day)
- *No Professional Shoots*
