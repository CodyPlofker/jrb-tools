Phase 1 — Research
Audience Research
Begin with output of angles and insights
▼

Research Activities

  • Push segments to Klaviyo and email specific "persona" surveys designed to enrich insights
  • Analyze reviews from this cohort to find shared insider language, desires, and "golden nugget" reviews for copy
  • Perform internet/Reddit, FB group research to understand desires, motivations, psychographics of persona
  • Listenlabs survey to understand the persona more
  • Create IG account and build it/interact as the persona
  • Scan FB Ads Library for non-competitive brands who successfully market to this persona
  • Use TikTok trends data to find top TikToks for key topics (Ex: this angle/topic is trending)

End Output: 1-Pager / Persona Brief

  • Who they are
  • Professions
  • Demographics
  • Psychographics
  • Key motivations and desires
  • Why our brand appeals
  • 4-6 core value props/angles to test
  • Creator profiles this persona follows
  • Creators that fit this persona
  • Successful non-competitive ads
  • Golden nugget reviews
  • Sample customer profiles

Phase 2 — MVP Testing
Static Production & Testing
Launch 12-50 diversified statics
▼

Goal

Launch enough assets to rule out execution as barrier to success while keeping initial cost low enough to not create risk.

Testing Parameters

  • Launch a toolkit of 12-50 diversified statics
  • Test minimum 4-5 unique angles or value props across diversified yet proven formats
  • Test 2-4 products per initial persona batch
  • Direct traffic to generic landing page or PDP
  • Launch one ad set per persona and product

Decision Thresholds (within 5-7 days)

  • Green Light: $2k/day on a concept at 0.8 ROAS or higher at 60% New Visit Rate
  • Yellow Light: $1k/day on a concept at 0.6 ROAS or higher at 50% New Visit Rate
  • Red Light: Minimal spend on a concept or <0.5 ROAS and/or <40% New Visit Rate
