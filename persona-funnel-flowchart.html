Decision Thresholds

Green Light

  • $2k/day on a concept
  • ≥ 0.8 ROAS
  • ≥ 60% New Visit Rate
  • → Scale to Phase 3

Yellow Light

  • $1k/day on a concept
  • ≥ 0.6 ROAS
  • ≥ 50% New Visit Rate
  • → Iterate & retest

Red Light

  • Minimal spend on concept
  • < 0.5 ROAS
  • < 40% New Visit Rate
  • → Cut & move on
3
Scaling, Iterating, and Cutting
Green = Scale, Yellow = Iterate, Red = Cut
▼

Green = Scaling

  • Scale winners within testing ad set and to other relevant campaigns
  • Produce second batch of statics, doubling down on winning concepts and format
  • Create batches for more specific sub-personas with exploratory formats
  • Animate statics into GIFs and short videos to drive new placements
  • Experiment with varying levels of awareness and sophistication
  • Experiment with unique mechanisms of the problem and solutions
  • Graduate to next phase of production

Yellow = Iterating

  • Refine angle/messaging
  • Test new creative formats
  • Adjust targeting parameters
  • Re-test with variations
  • Consider sub-segments
  • Review and optimize

Red = Cutting

  • Cut losses
  • Move on from that concept or format
  • Document learnings
  • Apply insights to other personas
↓
If Green → Continue to Phase 4
